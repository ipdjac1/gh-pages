background-attachment

Applicable to the body of the entire page. Usage:

background-image:url(turtle.jpg);
background-repeat:no-repeat;
background-position: top center;
background-attachment:fixed;

scroll - Background image scrolls with the page. fixed - Background image does not scroll with the page.

background-position

background-position:top right;

background-shorthand

background: [color] [image] [position] [repeat] [attachment]
